# Nueva Maya Alphabet

## Consonants — plain stops & fricatives

| Nueva Maya | IPA | Sounds like |
|-----------|-----|-------------|
| p | /p/ | p in *spin* |
| t | /t/ | t in *stop* |
| k | /k/ | k in *sky* |
| b | /ɓ/ | b with slight inward breath (implosive) |
| s | /s/ | s in *sun* |
| x | /ʃ/ | sh in *she* |
| j | /χ/ | ch in Scottish *loch* |
| r | /r/ | r in Spanish (loanwords only) |

## Consonants — ejectives (replacing ALMG apostrophe)

| Nueva Maya | IPA | ALMG | Sounds like |
|-----------|-----|------|-------------|
| f | /pʼ/ | p' | p! — sharp popping release |
| d | /tʼ/ | t' | t! — sharp dental pop |
| g | /kʼ/ | k' | k! — velar pop at throat |
| c | /t͡ʃʼ/ | ch' | ch! — ejective palatal |
| dz | /t͡sʼ/ | ts' | ts! — ejective alveolar |

## Affricates

| Nueva Maya | IPA | ALMG | Sounds like |
|-----------|-----|------|-------------|
| ch | /t͡ʃ/ | ch | ch in *chair* |
| ts | /t͡s/ | ts | ts in *pizza* |

## Glottal stop (replacing ALMG saltillo)

| Nueva Maya | IPA | ALMG | Position |
|-----------|-----|------|----------|
| h | /ʔ/ | ' | syllable onset or vowel coda |
| -h | /Vʔ/ | V' | after vowel: `ah` `eeh` `iih` |

## Vowels — 5 base × 5 forms

| Form | Pattern | Example | Meaning |
|------|---------|---------|---------|
| Short | `a e i o u` | `bak` | bone |
| Long | `aa ee ii oo uu` | `kaan` | sky |
| Long high-tone | `áa ée íi óo úu` | `gíin` | sun |
| Glottal coda | `ah eh ih oh uh` | `maayah` | Maya |
| Long glottal | `aah eeh iih ooh uuh` | `uuh` | plural |

## Tone

| Mark | Tone | Example |
|------|------|---------|
| no mark | neutral | `bak` |
| acute accent | high `á` | `gíin` — sun |
| grave accent | low `à` | `daàhn` — speech (low) |

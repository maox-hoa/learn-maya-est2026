# Lesson 01 — Answers

## Exercise 1 — Translate to Nueva Maya

1. Good morning.  
   → **`Malob jahat giin`**

2. My name is _____.  
   → **`In gaabah e _____`**

3. Very nice to meet you.  
   → **`Jach giimag in wool in wiligech`**

4. Thank you.  
   → **`Kiito bohodtig`** *(Finnish-infused modern reform)*

5. Goodbye.  
   → **`Ka gaaht`**

---

## Exercise 2 — Translate to English

1. `Bix a beel?`  
   → **How are you?** *(lit. "how is your road?")*

2. `In gaabah e Balaam.`  
   → **My name is Balaam.**

3. `Ma tin nahatik.`  
   → **I don't understand.**

4. `A taatah Maayah winik wah?`  
   → **Is your father a Maya person?**

5. `Jach mahalob, nib óolal.`  
   → **Very good, you're welcome.**

## Exercise 3 — Fill in the blank

1. `_____ a beel?` (How)  
   → **`Bix`**

2. `In gaabah _____ Ixchel.` (is)  
   → **`e`**

3. `Mahalob, _____ óolal.` (you're welcome)  
   → **`nib`**

4. `_____ mahalob.` (very)  
   → **`Jach`**

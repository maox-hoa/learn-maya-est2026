## Key phrases

### Greetings

| Nueva Maya | English |
|-----------|---------|
| Bix a beel? | How are you? (lit. "how is your road?") |
| Gíimag óolal | Welcome |
| Mahalob | Good / Fine |
| Malob jahat giin | Good morning |
| Malob chinil giin | Good afternoon |
| Malob agabtal | Good evening |
| Ka gaaht | Goodbye |

> **Cultural note:** Maya has no direct word for "hello."
> `Bix a beel?` — *"how is your road?"* — reflects a
> culture of travelers and traders for whom asking about
> someone's journey was more meaningful than a formulaic greeting.

### Politeness

| Nueva Maya | English |
|-----------|---------|
